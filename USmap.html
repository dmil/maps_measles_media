Measles cases in the US by state

Percentage of children with exemptions from vaccine requirements

US state laws require children to have certain vaccines as a prerequisite for entry into daycare and kindergarden. All 50 state allow for medical exemptions from this requirement. Studies have shown that vaccine exemptions tend to cluster geographically, rendering some cummunities particularly vulnerable to outbreaks (Wang et al). Most states (45 and Washington DC) also grant religious exemptions and 15 states allow philosophical exemptions based on other non-religous personal or moral belief (Source: National Conference of State Legislatures, drawing on LexisNexis StateNet Database and the Immunization Action Coalition, May 2019).

This grid map is based on data from the 2018-2019 School Year Vaccination Exemptions Reports published by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. It shows the estimated percentage of children enrolled in kindergarden in different US states with an exemption from one or more vaccines during the 2018-2019 school year. The key limitations of the data are that (i) no data is available for the densely-populated DC area and (ii) exemptions are not broken down by vaccine type. Data source

We chose a grid chloropleth map to represent this data, where all states are represented by identical squares irrespective of their shape and size, rather than a traditional geographic map. This is because the latter may give rise to misleading interpretations of data, as small states are barely discernible and large states capture most of the reader's attention. The grid map is based on Gerald Rich's code. There are a number of different ways to arrange the squares in a grid map of the US and a range of different accuracy metrics by which to measure them. We changed the order of the squares in the original code to replicate the New York Times' grid map which is particularly good at ensuring that neighboring states in real life continue to be neighboring states in the visualization. This is important from a public health perspective as infectious disease can spread across state lines. Template source code Our source code Learn more about how to choose grid maps
